Habit Tracker - Privacy Policy

We collect and store:

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to provide app functionality and is not shared with third parties.

Account & Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by:

  • Email: Send a request to a@atraj.it

Deletion will be completed within 30 days.
